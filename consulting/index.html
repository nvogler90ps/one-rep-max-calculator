About

Nicholas Vogler is a machine learning engineer who helps teams go from AI proof-of-concept to production system -- building integrations that are scalable, maintainable, and actually useful.

Before tech, he served 5.5 years as a Sergeant in the US Marine Corps -- managing networks and data systems across deployments in Germany and Pakistan. That military discipline carries into everything he builds.

With a CS degree from Michigan and a Master's in Data Science from UC Berkeley, he has spent 15+ years building data and ML systems across defense, intelligence, and commercial sectors -- from computer vision pipelines to large-scale data infrastructure (including a US patent for data extraction).

Through Automated Insights LLC, he works with teams to design AI-powered features, architect reliable ML pipelines, and ensure that AI investments translate into durable, well-engineered products rather than fragile demos.
