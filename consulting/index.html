About

Nicholas Vogler is a machine learning engineer and data architect with experience spanning defense, intelligence, healthcare, and commercial ML systems.

After serving as a Marine Corps Sergeant -- managing data systems and networks across three continents -- he earned a BS in Computer Science from the University of Michigan and a Master's in Data Science from UC Berkeley.

His career includes building data extraction pipelines at Northrop Grumman (resulting in a US patent), engineering ML infrastructure at Striveworks, and contributing to published research on diabetes prevention at the VA.

Through Automated Insights LLC, he brings this cross-domain expertise to organizations building data-intensive systems where reliability and security are non-negotiable.
