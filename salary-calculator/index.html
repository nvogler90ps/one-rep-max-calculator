Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert salary to hourly rate?

Divide your annual salary by the total working hours in a year. With 40 hours/week and 52 weeks/year, that is 2,080 hours. For example, $50,000 / 2,080 = $24.04/hour.

How do I convert hourly rate to annual salary?

Multiply your hourly rate by hours per week, then by weeks per year. For example, $20/hour x 40 hours x 52 weeks = $41,600/year.

Are the tax estimates accurate?

The calculator uses 2026 federal income tax brackets for single filers as a rough estimate. Actual taxes depend on filing status, deductions, state taxes, FICA, and other factors. Consult a tax professional for precise calculations.

Why does the calculator default to 2,080 hours?

2,080 is the standard full-time work year: 40 hours/week x 52 weeks. You can adjust both values to match your actual schedule, including part-time work or fewer weeks for unpaid time off.
