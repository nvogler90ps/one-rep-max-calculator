V-Bucks Pricing Guide: How to Get the Best Value

How V-Bucks Pricing Works

V-Bucks are the in-game currency for Fortnite, used to purchase cosmetic items like skins, emotes, wraps, and Battle Passes. Epic Games sells V-Bucks in four bundle tiers, with larger bundles offering a lower cost per V-Buck. Since Epic's 2025 price increase, V-Bucks cost more than they used to -- making it even more important to buy the right bundle.

Prices vary by region due to local purchasing power, currency exchange rates, and regional pricing agreements. Some countries pay significantly less (or more) than the US base price when converted to USD.

Best Value Tips

  • Always buy the largest bundle you can afford. The 13,500 V-Bucks bundle saves roughly 26% per V-Buck compared to buying 1,000 at a time.
  • Wait for the Fortnite Crew subscription. At $11.99/month, Crew includes 1,000 V-Bucks, the Battle Pass, and exclusive cosmetics -- often the best deal if you play regularly.
  • Earn free V-Bucks through Save the World daily quests, Battle Pass progression, and seasonal events before spending money.
  • Check regional pricing before purchasing. If you travel or have accounts in different regions, prices can differ substantially.

Understanding Skin Rarity and Pricing

Fortnite cosmetics are priced by rarity tier. Uncommon skins cost 500 V-Bucks (roughly $4.00 USD at the best bundle rate), while Legendary skins range from 1,500 to 2,000 V-Bucks ($8.88 to $11.85 USD). Bundles with multiple items can exceed 2,500 V-Bucks.

Keep in mind that the real-money cost of a skin depends on which V-Bucks bundle you buy. A 1,500 V-Bucks Legendary skin costs $11.99 if you buy V-Bucks in the smallest bundle, but only $8.88 with the largest bundle -- a savings of over $3 per skin.

2025 Price Increase Impact

In 2025, Epic Games raised V-Bucks prices across most regions, bringing them in line with industry standards for in-game currency. The US saw increases of roughly 15-20% across all bundle tiers. This means buying strategically -- choosing the right bundle size, taking advantage of Crew, and earning free V-Bucks -- matters more than ever.

Regional prices were also adjusted, with some countries seeing larger increases than others. Use the regional comparison tool above to see the current equivalent prices in your country and find the best deal available to you.
